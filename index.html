London's Tech Sector Survey

Thank you for taking the time to participate in this survey!

The City of London and the Data Visualization program at Fanshawe College have partnered on a project designed to provide a 'snapshot' of the London digital creative community, through a selection of relevant interactive visualizations and dashboards. The target is a Web application with a back end that could be used to continue to gather new and changing data in future. The information we are collecting in this phase will help shape the project as it develops.

The City wants to collect and visualize information about digital creative companies, as they want to use the information as part of their ongoing planning activities, from selecting good locations for possible incubator spaces, to gather some insights into the digital creative industry moving out of the first COVID lockdown, and to identifying actions to help support and grow the sector.

Importantly, the information gathered will allow development of an application that can more fully represent the strength and breadth of the digital creative sector in London. The City wants this application to be a step towards better representation of London worldwide as a vital and growing tech hub.

Notes:

The information collected in this study will be visualized at the company level for general demographic information (number of employees etc.) All other information pertaining to trends, employee skill sets, etc. will be presented in a summarized fashion per sector, with no specific company or individual information. For questions related to the COVID first wave, for the purposes of this survey we have defined 3 time periods:

  1. December 2019 to March 2020 (Pre-COVID)
  2. March to July 2020 (Lockdown)
  3. August to December 2020 (Initial Recovery Period)
